#**Case Description** 
#The Live Music Circuit (LMC) is a company that helps organize concerts around Athens, Georgia by managing venues, artists, events, and equipment. They keep basic information on venues (like name, location, and capacity) and artists (like name and contact info), and they also track relationships between venues, such as shared ownership or equipment, including past partnerships. Each concert, or show event, takes place at one venue on a specific date and time, and includes one or more artists performing in a set order. Every event has a main coordinator and a backup staff member. LMC also manages equipment like speakers and lighting, where each item has its own ID and condition, but pricing is based on the type of equipment and whether the venue is a partner. Equipment can be assigned to events with scheduled usage times, and the same item cannot be used for overlapping events. Overall, events and equipment are related but flexible—events can happen without LMC equipment, and equipment can also be used for non-concert purposes.
